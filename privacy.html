Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

This policy outlines the privacy practices for Version Control (The Game) and ensures compliance with data protection standards.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data, account information, or biometric data from our users. The app functions entirely without the need for external data transmission to Labworks Games.

2. Use of Data

Any data generated by the application—such as game progress, locally saved settings, and narrative variables—is used solely to facilitate the core gameplay experience on your device. This data is stored locally and is not used for marketing, tracking, or third-party profiling.

3. Data Deletion & Requests

Since all game data is stored locally on your device, you may delete your data at any time by:

  • Manually deleting the local save directory from your device storage.
  • Uninstalling the application from your device.

If you have questions regarding data management or wish to confirm that no data is being held, you may contact our team via our email, at admin@labworksgames.com

4. Data Protection

Labworks Games maintains strict internal standards to ensure our application remains secure.
